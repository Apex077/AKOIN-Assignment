Who We Are

An Industry-Led Institute
Built for High-Stakes Environments

The Institute of Digital Risk is a specialist training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber and technology risk. We exist to close the critical skills gap in regulated, high-consequence sectors such as financial services, critical national infrastructure, healthcare, and government through developing practitioners who combine theoretical rigour with hands-on capability.

Our model anchors academic insight through a UK university partnership, ensuring our curriculum is evidence-based and current. Graduates and professionals don't just learn — they are deployed on real projects and leave IDR as credentialled, job-ready risk practitioners.
